# Adidas-Sales
# Adidas Sales Performance Analysis (2020-2021)
## Project Overview
This project provides a data-driven deep dive into Adidas' financial performance over a two-year period. It analyzes sales trends, product profitability, and retailer effectiveness to identify growth drivers and provide strategic business recommendations.The analysis highlights a period of explosive growth (324% Profit Growth) and identifies high-potential segments like Women's Apparel while underscoring the dominance of the West region.
## Technical Stack & Skills
- Data Cleaning: Power Query (M Language) used for ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes.
- Data Modeling: Star Schema design for optimized dashboard performance.
- Calculations: Advanced DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom KPIs and Time Intelligence.
- Visualization: Power BI Desktop.
## Key Findings
1. Financial Growth
   2021 Sales: $71.78M (Up 294% from 2020).
   2021 Profit: $26.88M (Up 324% from 2020).
   Profitability: Strong 42.30% average profit margin, indicating effective cost management.
2. Product Insights
   Top Performer: Men's Street Footwear leads in volume (593K units) and total profit.
   High Growth Outlier: Women's Apparel is the fastest-growing category, with profit growth peaking over 400%.
3. Retailer Distribution
   Concentration: 72% of total volume is driven by the top 3 retailers: West Gear, Foot Locker, and Sports Direct.
   West Gear stands as the primary profit contributor with $24.30M in total sales.

### Measures created include:
- Sales Growth % (YOY Growth)
- Profit Growth %
- Total Sold Units
